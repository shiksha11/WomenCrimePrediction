Women Crime Prediction


Prediction of the probability of crime in any region is a serious problem, especially in developing countries. Several factors can influence the crime rate at any location. Till date, there have been no effective solutions and dedicated research study towards the increasing crime cases, especially towards women. Thus, we have trained a machine learning model in order to come up with the best probability prediction model for different crime types. The data used was gathered from 50 districts in Madhya Pradesh, India. Go ahead and try our predictor. 😎
